How it works

Ideas, forged in The Crucible.

You watch it happen in real time — or leave it on auto-pilot and check the winners later.

01 · The Crucible

Three agents debate

A Researcher scopes the market, a Pitch makes the case, and a Critic tears in — arguing until the idea is convinced or killed.

02 · Graduated

Winners graduate

Ideas that survive the debate get a full plan and land in your Graduated board, ready to build.

03 · Workroom

Agents build it

A Planner and Executor pair produce real deliverables for each idea — researching live on the web and checking each other's work.

04 · Master Inbox

You just approve

Only what genuinely needs a human — pay, sign off, send, decide — surfaces to you. The agents handle the rest.
